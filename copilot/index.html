FAQ

Questions, answered

The essentials about how LoveAds Copilot works.

What is LoveAds Copilot?

LoveAds Copilot is a profit-analytics platform that connects your Google Ads, Meta Ads and Shopify in one place and tells you what's making money, what's leaking it, and exactly what to do next — in plain language, not dashboards.

What is POAS and how is it different from ROAS?

POAS (Profit on Ad Spend) is revenue minus real product cost, divided by ad spend. Unlike ROAS, which counts revenue before costs, POAS reflects actual profit — so you optimize for money in the bank rather than an inflated number.

Does Copilot change my campaigns or store?

No. Copilot is read-only and secure. It connects via OAuth to read your ad and store data so it can advise you, but it never edits your campaigns, products, or checkout.

Which channels does LoveAds Copilot support?

Google Ads, Meta Ads and Shopify are supported, with de-duplicated profit so the same sale isn't counted twice. TikTok and Creative Intelligence are available on the Scale and Pro tiers.

How much does LoveAds Copilot cost?

Plans start at €99/month (Founding, price locked for life for the first 100 customers), then Growth €199/month, Scale €399/month and Pro €799/month. All plans include a 14-day trial.

E-commerce brands and store owners running their own Google, Meta and Shopify — who are not necessarily PPC experts — and marketing agencies managing many clients who need a portfolio view plus per-client intelligence.
