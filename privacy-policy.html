Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025  ·  Love Ads Marketing SRL

Love Ads Marketing SRL ("we", "us" or "our") operates the website www.loveads.ro. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Contact information — name, email address, phone number when you submit a contact form.
  • Usage data — IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, referral URLs. This data is collected automatically via analytics tools.
  • Cookies and tracking data — see Section 6 below.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to enquiries and contact form submissions.
  • Improve and maintain the website.
  • Analyse usage patterns to enhance user experience.
  • Comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area, we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contractual necessity — to respond to your enquiries.
  • Legitimate interests — to improve our website and services.
  • Legal obligation — where required by law.
  • Consent — for cookies and marketing communications where applicable.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers — third-party vendors who assist in operating our website and services (e.g. email delivery, analytics), subject to appropriate data processing agreements.
  • Legal authorities — where required by applicable law or court order.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law. Contact form submissions are retained for up to 12 months unless a longer retention period is required.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience. We use:

  • Essential cookies — required for the website to function correctly.
  • Analytics cookies — via Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager to understand how visitors interact with the site.

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the website.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR and Romanian data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request erasure of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Object to or restrict our processing of your data.
  • Request data portability.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent).
  • Lodge a complaint with the Romanian National Supervisory Authority (ANSPDCP) at www.dataprotection.ro.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at sebastian.cosmor@loveads.ro.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.
